<div class="tutorial-steps">

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">1</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Open Marker in your browser</h3>
<p>Go to <strong>app.marker.golf</strong> in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android). Marker is a progressive web app — no App Store download required, though it is also available in the App Store and Google Play.</p>
<p>For the best experience on iPhone, use Safari. On Android, use Chrome. Other browsers work but may not support home screen installation.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">2</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Sign in with your phone number</h3>
<p>Marker uses phone number authentication — no password, no email required. Enter your mobile number and tap <strong>Send Code</strong>. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code by SMS within a few seconds.</p>
<p>Enter the code on the next screen and tap <strong>Verify</strong>. You're now signed in. Your number is your identity in Marker — other players can find and invite you by it.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">3</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Set up your profile</h3>
<p>On first sign-in you'll be prompted to enter your name. Use your real name so other players can find you easily when they're building their group.</p>
<p>You can also add your GHIN handicap index here if you have one — this lets Marker calculate course handicaps automatically for supported game formats. You can always add or update this later in your profile settings.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">4</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Install Marker on your home screen</h3>
<p><strong>iPhone (Safari):</strong> Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the browser, then scroll down and tap <em>Add to Home Screen</em>. Give it a name and tap <em>Add</em>.</p>
<p><strong>Android (Chrome):</strong> Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner and select <em>Add to Home Screen</em>. Tap <em>Add</em> to confirm.</p>
<p>Once installed, Marker opens full-screen like a native app — no browser chrome, no address bar. This is the best way to use Marker on the course.</p>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="tutorial-note">
<p><strong>Returning players:</strong> If you've been added to someone else's event, open the SMS link they sent you. It will sign you in automatically and take you straight to the event.</p>
</div>
