<div class="tutorial-steps">

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">1</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Open the live leaderboard</h3>
<p>From your event dashboard, tap the <strong>Trophy icon</strong> (or the Leaderboard tab). This shows the real-time standings for all players across all groups in the event — sorted by net score, points, or game-specific ranking depending on the format you're running.</p>
<p>The leaderboard updates automatically as players enter scores on their scorecards. No refreshing required.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">2</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Track scores across groups</h3>
<p>The event leaderboard aggregates every group's scorecard. You can see where everyone stands even if they're on different holes. Tap any player's name to see their hole-by-hole scorecard in full.</p>
<p>For multi-game events, the leaderboard can show separate standings for each active game. Swipe or tap to switch between game views.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">3</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Settle up after the round</h3>
<p>Once all scores are in, tap <strong>Settle</strong> from the event dashboard. Marker calculates the final payouts for every active game — skins, Nassau, stroke play net, Stableford points, and more — and shows a clear breakdown of who owes what to whom.</p>
<p>Payouts are displayed as net amounts. For example, if Player A owes Player B $10 and Player C $5, but Player C owes Player A $3, Marker simplifies this to the minimum number of transactions needed to settle all debts.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">4</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Record results for a series</h3>
<p>If you run a regular group — weekly game, league, or club series — Marker can track cumulative results across multiple events. After settling, tap <strong>Save to Series</strong> to log the result. The series leaderboard shows running totals over the season.</p>
<p>Series tracking lets you run a season-long competition without any separate spreadsheet or record-keeping.</p>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="tutorial-note">
<p><strong>Sharing the leaderboard:</strong> Tap the Share icon from the leaderboard to copy a link. Anyone with the link can view the live standings in their browser — no Marker account needed. Great for spectators following along from the clubhouse.</p>
</div>
