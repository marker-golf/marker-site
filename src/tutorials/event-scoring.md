<div class="tutorial-steps">

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">1</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Publish Scorecards</h3>
<p>When your field is set and scorecards are organized, tap <strong>Publish Scorecards</strong> from the event dashboard. This sends each player a link to their individual scorecard. Players can open the link on any phone — no Marker account required to enter scores or view the leaderboard.</p>
<p>There is no separate "Start Event" action. Publishing is the step that distributes scorecards to players and opens the event for scoring.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">2</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Follow live standings</h3>
<p>From your event dashboard, tap the <strong>Trophy icon</strong> to open the event leaderboard. This shows standings for all players across all groups — sorted by net score, points, or game-specific ranking depending on the format. A separate leaderboard card is shown for each active event game.</p>
<p>The leaderboard reflects scores as players enter them. Tap any player's name to see their hole-by-hole scorecard in full.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">3</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Track scores across groups</h3>
<p>The event leaderboard aggregates every group's scorecard. You can see where everyone stands even if they're on different holes. For multi-game events, separate leaderboard cards show standings for each active game.</p>
<p>Any player with a scorecard link can also view the event leaderboard — commissioner and players see the same live standings.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">4</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Complete the event</h3>
<p>When all scores are in, tap <strong>Complete Event</strong> from the event dashboard. Marker confirms before locking. Completing the event locks all scorecards and freezes game results.</p>
<p>You can unlock the event afterward from event settings if you need to make a correction.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">5</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Settle up</h3>
<p>Event settlement is available on the leaderboard screen once the event is complete. The settlement view shows the combined result across all event games.</p>
<p>Toggle <strong>Buy-ins collected</strong> to switch between two settlement modes: <em>pot-based</em> (winnings distributed from a collected pot) and <em>direct</em> (player-to-player settlement). The <strong>Profit/Loss</strong> tab shows each player's gross winnings, buy-in cost, and net result. Tap the share icon to send the leaderboard link to the group.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">6</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Record results for a series</h3>
<p>If your event is part of a series, tap <strong>Save to Series</strong> after settling. The series leaderboard tracks cumulative results across all events in the group — useful for league seasons, club trips, or any regular competition where you want season-long standings.</p>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="tutorial-note">
<p><strong>Sharing the leaderboard:</strong> Tap the Share icon from the leaderboard to copy a link. Anyone with the link can view the live standings in their browser — no Marker account needed. Great for spectators or members of the club following along.</p>
</div>
