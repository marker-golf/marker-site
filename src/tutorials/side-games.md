<div class="tutorial-steps">

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">1</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>What is a side game?</h3>
<p>A side game is a match that runs across scorecards in different groups. For example, if you and your partner are in Cart 1 and your opponents are in Cart 2, you can run a Nassau between the two carts — even though you're not playing hole-for-hole together.</p>
<p>Marker calculates the result using each player's entered scores. Both sides see live standings as they play, and settlement is automatic at the end.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">2</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Open the scorecard info button</h3>
<p>From your scorecard, tap the <strong>Info button</strong> (the ⓘ icon, usually in the top-right corner). This opens the scorecard options panel where you can configure games and side matches for your group.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">3</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Add a side match</h3>
<p>Tap <strong>Side Match</strong> or <strong>Add Side Game</strong>. Select the game format (Nassau is the most common for cross-group play). Then select which players from your group are in the match and which players from the other group they're competing against.</p>
<p>You can configure 1v1 matches, 2v2 team matches, or individual vs. individual across groups.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">4</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Configure the match settings</h3>
<p>Set the stake amount and any game-specific options (e.g. presses for Nassau, carryover rules for Skins). Marker will confirm the match with both sides — the opposing players will see the side match appear on their scorecard automatically.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="tutorial-step">
<div class="tutorial-step__marker" aria-hidden="true">5</div>
<div class="tutorial-step__content">
<h3>Track and settle</h3>
<p>As both groups enter scores hole by hole, Marker updates the side match standings in real time. You'll see how the match stands after every hole without waiting to compare cards at the end.</p>
<p>When the round is over, the side match result feeds into the main event settlement. One settle screen, all games, all groups.</p>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="tutorial-note">
<p><strong>Cross-group Nassau:</strong> The most popular side game on Marker. Two players in different carts bet on front 9, back 9, and overall 18 — Marker tracks all three bets automatically and handles press rules without any manual tracking.</p>
</div>
